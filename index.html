Words are never enough

Simple but never dead
The words left hanging in the air,
Unable to catch them,
They fall to the ground.
Buried deep inside not to live,
Not to die but remain unknown,
Hidden from people left alone,
Covered in a blanket
Not long ago, left behind.

I still dream of you,
Not too much,
But sometimes, when it rains,
My mind wanders to your home,
Thinking of you.

Man

His eyes burn with rage—
trying to strangle the crowd
with nothing but his looks.

He's angry.
They never understood him,
mocked him,
beat him down.

He is a man.
He is a god.
He is human.

But he is nothing,
nothing but flesh and dirt.

And he will return to the dirt.

In the end, nothing matters.
Everything will be forgotten.

Love

It's not love
if you're constantly thinking about them—
that's obsession.
They're two very different entities.
Love is caring for the person,
not for who they are
or what they are.
People have changed the meaning of love
with their petty games.
Even the kids these days say,
"I manipulate her/him,
I can make them cry whenever I want."
"They're weak,
I can do anything I want to them."
In truth, love never asks for anything in return—
not even the love they gave you.
The most grateful creatures
are those who seek love,
but never get anything.
Such pitiful, miserable souls.

She is......

She is the poem unwritten,
A melody that can be found anywhere.
More beauty, more heart than any words said,
A kind soul in between these filthy mortals.
Her presence brightens one's day;
Her eyes can change from sad to happy,
Turning tears into laughter.

She is a blessing to have.
She helps others before caring for herself.
She is kind, looks after her parents;
That kindness may hurt her,
But she doesn't abandon that kindness.

She sees her parents' struggles
And helps them to her best.
Her wish is to make them the proudest parents on earth.
She loves her friends
And tries to make them better.

Sometimes she acts dumb,
Like she doubts herself,
That she won't succeed,
Always worries
That what she's done
Isn't enough.
She pushes through with her little strength.
She persists, she fights,
And she gets what she wants;
Sometimes she even achieves the impossible.

She says
She is not beautiful.
True, she's not a model,
But that doesn't mean she's not perfect.
The way those eyes shine
Like earth kissed by spring rain,
Promising life and happiness,
Mirrored by her wide smile,
Though not often seen—
Just one smile from her
Is like a ray of sunshine
Through those broken windows,
Bringing light into darkness
That knew nothing but darkness,
Warm enough to melt the walls of the coldest hearts.

I say
She's often right,
But the subject
She becomes herself
And she's far from the truth.
She's absolutely wonderful,
A perfect person,
A perfect friend,
The most beautiful woman on earth.

My rose

A rose that grows in the shade
Beautiful but without light
It's thorns ready to tear flesh
While her eyes hold a thousand complaints

She smiles despite the pain
Hides her tears behind the scent
She wishes for escape
But fears losing her way

She is beautiful her delicate grace
Her heart is heavy
Waiting for sun that is yet to come
Like a distant dream

In the quiet night, the sky calls
They whisper about her among the clouds
Whisper about hope
That tomorrow might shine despite the clouds.

No-name

I’ve met so many people,
But when I close my eyes,
It’s always those brown eyes I see,
Still holding on to something, God knows what.
Because it’s you,
And it will always be you.

You held my hand and promised forever,
I thought I was foolish to believe,
But that was more than I could have ever wished for.

You kissed my lips softly
And all I could think of
Was the way we could be if we just let go,
A perfect picture of love
Painted with every moment we shared.

But those moments faded like a forgotten dream,
Chased away by the noise of the world
Until all that’s left is silence.

I’ve met so many people,
But when I close my eyes,
I always see you.

Alone am I?

Alone am I in this world,
A lonely island lost at sea,
The stars seem so far away,
Their light can barely reach me.

I gaze at the moon, a distant friend,
Who listens but cannot touch,
A silent witness to my thoughts,
My sorrows, and all that I clutch.

The wind carries whispers of the past,
Echoes of laughter, shadows of tears,
Memories that fade like smoke in the air,
Leaving me with my fears.

Will I find my way back,
To the warmth of a gentle embrace?
Or am I destined to wander,
This empty, desolate place?

Angel

She stood on the stage in her golden white dress,
Like an angel.
Her serene laughter echoed through the hall,
And I just stood there, watching her.
From afar, it was sweet yet painful,
Perhaps one day, we will stand next to each other

For my love

I just want to be loved by her.
Even though it feels impossible,
I've never thought of giving up.

If not today, or tomorrow,
maybe one day, we'll find love.
She will love me,
for we are destined to be together.

Right, my love?

Monster am I?

I live in a home with monsters
We laugh under the roof always giggling
Their eyes glow in the shadows, sharp teeth,
Their smiles are more wicked

The food like lava burns my tongue
And milk drips from the twisted trees
I hate living like a caged rat
Yet I love these weird monsters
Weird am I?

One day I'll take you to my house
Nestled deep in the forest
Where the trees whisper about me
And we will dance in the dark

Dear Me.....

I watch her dance in the pale moonlight,
Her wild heart that never stays.
Yet for her, the world stands still,
Her sigh breaks men like me.

In every glance, in every sigh,
She holds the world beneath her eyes.
A gentle hand, a steady will,
And when she smiles, the stars ignite.

For love is her, and she is love,
And in her eyes, I see it clear
All that I want, and all that’s dear.

Beauty

If the devil saw you,
he'd kiss your eyes
and might turn back to the Lord,
for in you, his creation is nothing but beautiful.
Even the angels are at a loss.
Those gleaming eyes,
and soft lips calling my name—
how could I forget such a thing?
I am just a man,
not a being who can resist the urge
to keep you close.
